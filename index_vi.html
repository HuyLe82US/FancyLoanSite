QM Loans

Conventional Loan

  • Requires proof of income
  • Competitive interest rates
  • Down payment as low as 3%-5%, depending on credit score
  • Credit score requirement: 620 or higher

VA Loan

  • Special loan for military personnel
  • No down payment required
  • Competitive interest rates

FHA Loan

  • For residential homes only
  • Down payment as low as 3.5%
  • Credit score requirement: 500 or higher
  • Competitive interest rates

USDA Loan

  • Designed for homebuyers in rural areas with benefits
    from the Department of Agriculture
  • No down payment required
  • Competitive interest rates

Non-QM Loan Programs

DSCR (Debt Service Coverage Ratio Loan)

  • No need to prove personal income
  • Uses rental income from the property being purchased to qualify for the loan
  • Credit score requirement: 660 or higher
  • Requires 12-24 months of bank statements
  • Income is calculated from bank statements without checking tax returns
  • Can use either personal or business bank accounts

P&L (Profit and Loss Loan)

  • No need to submit tax returns
  • Only requires a Profit & Loss statement from a CPA to verify income

ITIN Loan

  • Designed for individuals without a green card or U.S. citizenship
  • Requires proof of employment and tax filing with an ITIN (Individual Taxpayer Identification Number)

WVOE

  • abbr. Written Verification of Employment Loan
  • No need to submit tax returns
  • Requires only a written verification of income from the employer

No Ratio Loan

  • No need to prove income
  • No need to submit tax returns
  • Requires a down payment of 35%-40%

Foreigner Loan

  • Designed for foreign nationals who want to purchase property in the U.S. for investment
  • Requires a valid U.S. tourist visa
  • Must have a U.S. bank account to cover the down payment and closing costs
  • Allows closing to be signed at a U.S. consulate abroad
